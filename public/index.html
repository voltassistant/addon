üìã Required Home Assistant Sensors ‚ñº

VoltAssistant needs access to these sensors from your inverter integration:

  • sensor.battery_soc - Battery state of charge (%)
  • sensor.battery_power - Battery power flow (W)
  • sensor.grid_power - Grid import/export (W)
  • sensor.load_power - House consumption (W)
  • sensor.pv_power - Solar generation (W)
  • sensor.pvpc_price - Electricity price (‚Ç¨/kWh)
üîå Supported Inverters ‚ñº

Currently optimized for:

  • Deye SUN-xK-SG04LP3-EU series
  • Any inverter with Home Assistant integration exposing required sensors

The system uses HA services to control charging modes, so any inverter with proper HA integration should work.

üá™üá∏ Spanish 2.0TD Tariff Support ‚ñº

Full support for Spanish 2.0TD electricity tariff:

  • Valle (00-08h): Cheapest hours, optimal for charging
  • Llano (08-10h, 14-18h, 22-00h): Medium prices
  • Punta (10-14h, 18-22h): Peak prices, avoid charging

Configure different contracted power per period to manage load shedding correctly.
